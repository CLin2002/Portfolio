Hello! My name is Calvin Lin and I am a Product Designer

I love using visuals and prototypes to communicate ideas, and I believe that physical products are a universal language that everybody can understand. Growing up, I was always interested in assembling model cars and working with electronic components. I want to use my passion for designing products to better enrich the lives of those around me and create an impact in the global community.  At the moment, I am pursuing my undergraduate degree in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University with a concentration in design. However, at the moment, you can find me skiing on Cypress Mountain or finding new dessert spots in the lower mainland.
